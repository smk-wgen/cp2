The problem

The problem that we attempt to solve is to try and make your long commutes better. What if -
  • You could make friends
  • Network
  • Plan your commute

What we try to do

Give us your commute info - and we match you with other Linkedin users. You can then connect with them. Plan your commutes and network. Hopefully, interacting with a real human instead of being glued to your electronic device makes for a more enjoyable commute.
